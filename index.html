2024 Project - Shining Star
Demonstration of stars, constellation and exoplanets viewer for the CAVE2 system.

OVERVIEW

The Shining Star is a project where the user can see stars, exoplanets and constellation of different cultures in the CAVE2 360 display system. The user also has the freedom to move through space and see how the heavemly bodies move over vast spans of time.

APPROACH

Highlighted Optimization techniques:

  • GPU instanced rendering for the quad meshes
  • Backface culled billboarded quads instead of sphere for the stars
  • OctTree-based rendering mechanism to render only a subset of the stars at a given time
